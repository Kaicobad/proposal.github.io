5.4 Forecasting Algorithms (Included in Base Package)

Predictive Analytics Without AI

Our standard forecasting uses proven statistical algorithms that don't require AI/ML, keeping costs down while providing accurate predictions:

Sales Forecasting

  • Moving Average Method: Smooth out fluctuations to predict trends
  • Exponential Smoothing: Weighted averages giving more importance to recent data
  • Seasonal Decomposition: Identify and project seasonal patterns
  • Trend Analysis: Linear and polynomial trend projections
  • Time Horizons: 30-day, 60-day, 90-day forecasts

Demand Prediction

  • Historical Pattern Analysis: Based on past sales data
  • Seasonal Adjustments: Account for holidays and events
  • Product Lifecycle Tracking: Introduction, growth, maturity, decline phases
  • Category-level Forecasting: Predict demand by product category

Inventory Planning

  • Economic Order Quantity (EOQ): Optimal order quantities
  • Reorder Point Calculation: When to reorder based on lead time
  • Safety Stock Recommendations: Buffer stock for uncertainties
  • Stock Requirement Projections: Future inventory needs
